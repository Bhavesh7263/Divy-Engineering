Our Standards

Every product is tested, certified, and supplied with complete mill test reports to international standards.

  Home › Quality
Our Commitment

Quality at Every Stage

Raw Material Testing

All incoming raw materials are inspected with PMI and chemical analysis before production begins.

Dimensional Inspection

Every product is checked for dimensional accuracy — OD, wall thickness, length, and straightness per ASTM/EN standards.

Mechanical Testing

Tensile strength, yield strength, elongation, and hardness tests carried out per heat/lot in our in-house laboratory.

Non-Destructive Testing

Ultrasonic (UT), radiography (RT), and dye penetrant (DPT) inspection available as per client requirements.

Hydrostatic Testing

All pressure-bearing components hydro-tested to 1.5x operating pressure before despatch.

Mill Test Certificates

Every supply accompanied by EN 10204 3.1 or 3.2 Mill Test Reports traceable to the heat of manufacture.
